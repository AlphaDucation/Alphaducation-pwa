FIELD NOTES

Practitioner Field Notes

All Levels

Limitations & Cautions: Spacing does not replace first teaching. Students still need explanation, examples, and conceptual sense-making at first encounter. The evidence is stronger for retention of facts, procedures, and recognisable problem types than for proof, modelling, or far transfer, and the ideal spacing schedule still depends on context.
